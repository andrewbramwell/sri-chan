Thai Reflexology

Balance Your Body Through Pressure Points

Thai reflexology, also known as Thai foot reflexology or Thai foot massage, is a traditional healing practice that involves applying pressure to specific points on the feet.

It is based on the principles of reflexology, which suggest that these points correspond to different organs and systems in the body. By stimulating these reflex points, Thai reflexology aims to promote relaxation, improve circulation, balance energy, and support overall health. In Thai reflexology, the therapist uses thumbs, fingers, knuckles, and sometimes a specialised tool called a "stick" to press on reflex points.

  • Stress Relief

    Helps calm the nervous system, reduce stress, and promote deep relaxation by targeting specific reflex points.

  • Improved Circulation

    Stimulates blood flow throughout the body by applying pressure to specific reflex points on feet, hands, and ears.

  • Pain Relief

    Helps alleviate muscle tension, joint pain, and other discomforts by targeting reflex points linked to affected body areas.

  • Enhanced Energy

    Unblocks energy pathways in the body, potentially increasing energy levels and reducing fatigue.
