About Us

Our Story & Expertise

Meet May, the founder of Sri Chan Thai Massage, whose journey from Nakhon Ratchasima, Thailand to the UK has been guided by a deep passion for traditional healing arts. Growing up surrounded by Thai massage traditions, May developed an intuitive understanding of therapeutic touch that would later be refined through formal professional training. Since moving to the UK in 2010, she has dedicated herself to mastering both traditional Thai and Western massage techniques.

Sri Chan Thai Massage opened in December 2024 with a clear mission: to provide genuine therapeutic massage that promotes continuous body wellness and holistic healing. May's extensive qualifications and cultural heritage combine to create an authentic therapeutic experience that distinguishes Sri Chan from conventional massage practices.

My goal is elevating Thai massage reputation in the UK whilst providing genuine alternative treatment options. May Owner
