Aromatherapy Massage

Experience the Healing Power of Essential Oils

An aromatherapy massage combines traditional massage techniques with the use of essential oils derived from plants, flowers, and herbs to enhance physical and emotional well-being.

During the session, a therapist will typically blend specific oils tailored to your needs - whether for relaxation, energy, stress relief, or pain relief. The massage techniques are often gentle and relaxing, allowing the essential oils to be absorbed through the skin and inhaled, which may improve mood, relieve tension, and promote overall relaxation.

  • Stress relief

    Calming oils like lavender or chamomile can help reduce stress and promote mental relaxation.

  • Pain management

    Oils like peppermint and eucalyptus may help alleviate muscle aches and joint pain.

  • Enhanced circulation

    Massage combined with oils like rosemary can stimulate blood flow.

  • Improved mood

    Citrus oils like orange or lemon are often used to boost energy and mood.
